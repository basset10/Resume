Park Meetups

Park Meetups is an app that allows you to coordinate your dog's park visits with his or her best friends. One of the most important parts of raising well behaved dogs is socializing. You can check-in in real time as you arrive at the park or simply mark it on the calender when and where you and your furry friend will be.
