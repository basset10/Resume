In Loving Memory



02/27/2021

Everyone has always said once a photo is out on the internet, it's there forever. So I figured what better way to immortalize her than to create her own little doggy social media. Too many apps in todays climate are focused on censoring groups of people they don't agree with and pitting red versus blue or black versus white. This app won't do any of that. This app is for pet owners who just want to spread cute pics of their favorite pets. Science suggests that looking at these pictures is good for your health. Just animals along with their likes, hobbies, hopes and dreams. At least whatever we think that they would be. With depression on the rise, afflicting more than one third of US citizens, people need a platform where they aren't judged every second of the day.

Potential Apps

  • Walking Service - An app that connects you with people that will walk your dog for a small fee. Read walker reviews and pick the best one for your pet. I like to imagine Uber but for finding people to walk your pet.

  • Adoption Search - An app that scrapes data about pets up for adoption within a certain distance.

  • Park Meetups - Schedule and plan park meetups with your pets favorite friends.
